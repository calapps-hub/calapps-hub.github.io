How does CalFit calculate daily calorie needs?

We estimate your BMR based on height, weight, age, and activity level, then adjust for your goal to recommend daily intake.

Is my data private and secure?

Your data stays private and is never sold. You can export or delete it at any time from the app settings.

What features are included for free?

Free includes basic food database, daily logging, and reminders. Advanced features like barcode scan and weekly insights are available in Pro (coming soon).
